    About the FDP

    This FDP aims to provide fundamental practical knowledge to the research issues and applications in the upcoming area of Cloud Computing. The main objectives of this program include: The relevance of the FDP is to bring researchers, experts, students and faculty from academics fraternity on common platform to discuss share create awareness about the related technologies developments. It will benefit the faculty by providing them an opportunity to actively participate in research talks to be delivered by experts from across the country. Exposure to the related topics will help the faculty to enhance their skill knowledge in the field of Network Security and Cloud Computing. It will also enhance the research output of the faculty.
