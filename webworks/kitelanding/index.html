Why Choose Us?

INDUSTRY WITHIN & AROUND CAMPUS

With academia and industry in the same site, KiTE thrives in an IT-industry habitat inside the KGiSL campus, situated at the heart of Information Technology in the city, rendering an environment conducive for industry-embedded education. Get trained by industry experts

VALUE ADDITION COURSES

With our various industry collaborations, we offer a range of add-on courses that are relevant, trending and as up-to-date as the ever-evolving industry itself. KiTE immerses students in real-time learning to make them job-ready. Graduate as a sought-after engineer

KITE CULTURE – MORE GENIUS PER GENIUS

KiTE provides the right environment for students to nurture their curiosity and explore diverse skills and innovations through student-driven tech communities, workshops, coding challenges, rally car championships, hackathons and a host of events. Learn inside and beyond the classroom

About KiTE

KGiSL Institute of Technology is at the hub of the KGiSL Campus at Saravanampatti, with top-notch employers like Cognizant, NTT Data, KGiSL, TNQ and BOSCH as neighbours. Here, you will be exposed to cutting-edge developments and have opportunities for networking through professionals working in companies on our campus, excellent teachers and support staff, and guest speakers who are connected to the industry as researchers and consultants.
